Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q: How do I track my macros using the app?
    A: Enter the food items you consume, and the app will automatically calculate and display your macronutrient intake.
  • Q: Does the app offer meal recommendations?
    A: Yes, the app provides meal suggestions based on your macronutrient goals.
  • Q: Can I sync the app with other health tracking apps?
    A: Currently, syncing with other health apps is not supported, but we are working on adding this feature in future updates.
  • Q: Does the app store my food logs?
    A: Your food logs are stored locally on your device. We do not store or share any personal data.
  • Q: Is there a premium version?
    A: Yes, we offer a premium version with additional features such as advanced analytics and custom goal setting.
